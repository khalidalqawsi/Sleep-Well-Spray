What is SleepWell Spray?

SleepWell Spray is a pocket-sized aromatherapy spray designed to help you relax, unwind, and improve your sleep quality naturally.

Born from the everyday struggles of students facing stress, anxiety, and sleepless nights — our spray brings the calming power of essential oils directly to your pillow, bed, or surrounding space.

Easy to use, affordable, and sustainable with our refill option. Because everyone deserves a good night's sleep.
